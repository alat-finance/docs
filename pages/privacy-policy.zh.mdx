### Information:
At ALAT.FINANCE, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. As a censorship-resistant set of smart contracts deployed on Layer 1 chains, we are committed to ensuring the protection of your personal data. In connection with your use of our Services, we do not collect or store personal information such as first name, last name, street address, date of birth, email address, or IP address.

We may collect non-identifiable data, including public on-chain data and limited off-chain data such as device type, browser version and wallet type used. This data is solely used to enhance our production vision and does not involve tracking individual users.

If you voluntarily sign up to receive email, or other forms of communication from us, we will securely store your email address for the purpose of sending you those specific emails. You have the option to unsubscribe from these emails at any time. We assure you that we will not attempt, at any time, to link your email address to your wallet address, IP address, or any other personal data.

We encourage our users to take advantage of client-side privacy techniques and tools that empower them to further protect their privacy.

In the event of any significant changes to our privacy practices, we will update this privacy policy accordingly. It is important for you to review the policy periodically to stay informed about any modifications.
