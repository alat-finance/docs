## Logo Usage Guidelines

To maintain brand consistency and integrity, please adhere to the following guidelines when using the alat.finance logo:

- **Do not modify the logo**: Please use the provided logo files without altering its proportions, colors, or elements.
- **Maintain clear space**: Ensure that the logo has sufficient clear space around it to maintain visibility and legibility.
- **Use appropriate backgrounds**: Place the logo on a background that provides sufficient contrast to ensure visibility.
- **Do not stretch or distort**: Avoid stretching or distorting the logo. Use the provided files to maintain its original proportions.
- **Minimum size**: When scaling the logo, ensure that it is always legible. Use a minimum size of 40 pixels in digital applications.
